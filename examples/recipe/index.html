Spiced Lentil Soup with Crispy Shallots

A hearty, warming red lentil soup spiced with cumin and smoked paprika, finished with a drizzle of olive oil and crispy fried shallots. Ready in under 45 minutes.

Instructions

1

Sauté aromatics

Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook for 5 minutes until soft. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 more minute.

2

Toast spices

Add cumin, smoked paprika, turmeric, and coriander. Stir for 30 seconds until fragrant. This step is essential — don't skip it.

3

Add lentils and broth

Add rinsed red lentils, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 20–25 minutes until lentils are tender.

4

Blend and finish

Use an immersion blender to blend about half the soup for a creamy yet chunky texture. Season with salt and a squeeze of lemon juice.

5

Crispy shallots

Fry thinly sliced shallots in hot oil until golden. Drain on paper towels. Serve soup hot, topped with crispy shallots, fresh parsley, and a drizzle of chili oil.

💡 Pro tip

This soup freezes beautifully for up to 3 months. Make a double batch and freeze in portions for quick weeknight dinners.

Ingredients — serves 4

2 tbsp olive oil
1 large onion, diced
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 tbsp fresh ginger
2 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp smoked paprika
½ tsp turmeric
300g red lentils, rinsed
400g canned diced tomatoes
1.2L vegetable broth
Juice of 1 lemon
2 large shallots, thinly sliced
